About Us

Welcome to Dream Home, your number one source for all types of furniture. We're dedicated to giving you the very best of services, with a focus on dependability, customer service and uniqueness. Founded in 2011 by Jayden Miller, Dream Home has come a long way because of you support. Jayden Miller had a regular 9 to 5 job but he was always keen in starting some thing of his own. So, he quit his day job and with the support of his family and friends he decided to turn his creativity and hard work into a booming online store. We now serve customers all over the the world and are thrilled to be a part of the quirky, eco-friendly, fair trade wing of the furniture industry. We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.
